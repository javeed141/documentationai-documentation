---
title: Create Your First Project
description: Step-by-step guide to creating a new catapillar project.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Go to <a href="https://catapillar.io/login">catapillar.io/login</a> and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click on "New Project" at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter project name and optionally choose a template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create" to finish. Your project appears in the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
