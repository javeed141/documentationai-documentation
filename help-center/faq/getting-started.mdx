## Quick Start

To use catapillar:

1. Sign up at `https://catapillar.io/signup`.
2. Log in to your dashboard.
3. Click "New Project" to create your workspace.
4. Name your project and choose a template if needed.

Once your project is created, you can organize docs in the main dashboard.
