---
title: Create and Manage Loyalty Promotions
---

Creating and managing a loyalty promotion is a structured process that moves from initial configuration to final retirement. The following steps outline the complete journey, starting with how to create a new promotion by defining its details, audience, and earning rules. You can then approve or reject the loyalty promotion. Finally, you can pause or permanently end the loyalty promotion.

## **1. [Create a loyalty promotion](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion)**

#### i. [Define the promotion details](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion#step-1-defining-the-promotion-details)

Establish the promotion's core identity by defining its name, duration, and the loyalty program it belongs to.

#### ii. [Define who is eligible](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion#step-2-defining-who-is-eligible-enrolment)

Specify which customers are eligible to join the promotion, either by enroling all program members or targeting a specific segment.

#### iii. [Define how members join](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion#step-3-defining-how-members-join-optional-opt-in) *(optional)*

Configure an optional opt-in step that requires customers to perform an explicit action to actively join the promotion.

#### iv. [Define the earning activities](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion#step-4-defining-earning-activities)

Configure the core logic of the promotion by defining the specific actions a customer must complete to earn a reward.

#### v. [Configuring the promotion limits](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion#step-5-defining-the-promotion-limits-optional) *(optional)*

Apply limits to control the promotion's budget and scale by setting boundaries on participation and rewards.

#### vi. [Configuring the additional settings](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-creating-a-loyalty-promotion#step-6-configuring-additional-settings-optional) *(optional)*

Configure optional settings for internal reporting, financial tracking, and other operational needs.

***

## **2. [Approve or reject the loyalty promotion](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-approving-or-rejecting-a-loyalty-promotion)**

Perform the final quality review and either approve the promotion to make it live or reject it to return it to the maker for revisions.

***

## **3. [End the loyalty promotion](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-pausing-and-ending-a-loyalty-promotion)**

Manage the promotion's final state by either temporarily pausing it or permanently stopping it to archive the record.
