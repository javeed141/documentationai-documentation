## Viewing reports

To view or access a report, perform the following:

1. From the home page, navigate to Insights+. Click on **Home &gt; Insights+**.

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/616965d-Accessing_insights_for_viewing_reports.png" alt="" />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/1e469781-7441-415d-ab19-9fbf34e04777/7000682a-fc11-431f-8eb5-237c3d1212fa/images/ad3ef7ea-7e4a-4e4e-b0a2-675346e0195f.png" alt="" />

2. Expand the dropdown menu and select the organization for which you want to see the report.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/1e469781-7441-415d-ab19-9fbf34e04777/7000682a-fc11-431f-8eb5-237c3d1212fa/images/489c89a5-8a45-4a4d-830f-aa9fee4bb80f.png" alt="" />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/1e469781-7441-415d-ab19-9fbf34e04777/7000682a-fc11-431f-8eb5-237c3d1212fa/images/2545cdda-7337-4a38-a65b-4546445ec59a.png" alt="" />

3. Confirm the change.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/1e469781-7441-415d-ab19-9fbf34e04777/7000682a-fc11-431f-8eb5-237c3d1212fa/images/66c0def8-c541-451d-936d-700624439d0a.png" alt="" />

4. The Insights+ home page, by default, shows all the reports. Type the name of the desired report in the search box.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/a34df3be-5364-493f-91b4-983d25968fbe.png" alt="" />

2. To filter the report list using a specific KPI, chart type or report type, expand the corresponding dropdown and select the desired category.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/407dbb0b-4be9-4b40-9ba4-cc5c2613ffa0.png" alt="" />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/d792bec4-8cf4-4c51-850e-3cc1262f2912.png" alt="" />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/8e85f695-c7dd-450e-9c97-dd91e727fbf8.png" alt="" />

3. Click the desired report from the **Recently viewed** report section. Up to four reports are displayed.
4. To open any module-specific report, click and expand the module from the left navigation panel. For example, to see Loyalty+ reports, click on the dropdown menu and select the specific report. The desired report is displayed.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/65e9624c-3d3b-42a4-83f3-742f66168de9.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/b4d954ed-72d5-41a1-9947-cfc3d15f6dc5.png" alt="" />

5. To view reports created by you, click on **Created by Me** from the left navigation panel. All the reports that you have created are listed.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/a36db7e7-a021-46b6-8ba5-12a1241d24f8.png" alt="" />

6. To return to the full list of reports, click  **All Reports**.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/a50c0dcb-c33f-41a8-9c2e-6db29f8aa385.png" alt="" />

<br />

7. Add a report to your favourites list by clicking **Add to favourites**.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/6ad96931-a625-4f3f-9048-8b183632ae71/fe3f4ec8-5bb3-4ffd-b1ed-7500532abe6d/images/8f514248-e23e-4262-8b36-1574cb7f50f7.png" alt="" />

7. To view all your favourite reports, click the **Favourites** tab located in the left-hand navigation panel.
8. To sort the report list based on **Name**, **Category**, or **Last Updated** date, click on the desired tab. The sorted list is displayed.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/1e469781-7441-415d-ab19-9fbf34e04777/7000682a-fc11-431f-8eb5-237c3d1212fa/images/ce812f20-0f9a-4a97-aaf5-15b09f4f9a4b.png" alt="" />

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/dc13542-Sort_by_category.png" alt="" />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/1e469781-7441-415d-ab19-9fbf34e04777/7000682a-fc11-431f-8eb5-237c3d1212fa/images/277b7bb6-6819-4899-a72f-2f45845f0a66.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Callout kind="alert" title="WARNING">

A warning will appear if your org has selected 'Customer status' and 'Event user' filters while creating the chart. If customer status is enabled for your organisation while creating the chart:

* Make sure that you select all filters of **Event Users** and select the relevant filters under **Customer status**
* By default, customers with Deleted/ Internal/ Fraud confirmed are excluded from the report.

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/b51ae75-Warning_Customer_status.png" />

</Callout>

## Using various display options to view the report

Upon opening the report, you can use the following data display options.

1. To view a **KPI definition and Property**, open the desired report. Hover over the information icon next to the KPI to see the KPI definition and property.

   <br />

   <Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/a18f308a-f5dd-481a-b9ab-6f5917e9d017.png" alt="" />

   <br />

   <Image src="https://files.readme.io/c133de2-KPI_definition.png" alt="" />

Alternatively, click **More options &gt; View chart details &gt; KPI name** to see the KPI definition and property.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/10c86fdc-1eb7-4512-a061-36c07a6a908e.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/0c1b2e16-db83-47a4-b36b-a3b2ad68721d.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/0377fd15-8b66-4e05-907d-8332a6efb379.png" alt="" />

2. To see the data for a specific duration, click the **Calendar** icon. By default, the date filter shows the duration that was configured while creating the report. To know more about date filters, click [here](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/date-range-filter-comparison-with-previous-period-1).

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/799470c4-c95a-4cb5-a8a2-3529cfc40086.png" alt="" />

3. To filter the report data, click **Filters** and select the desired **dimensions/ attributes**. Click **Include** to apply the filter.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/4b0dd442-9eff-484c-bcfc-9c80bdc67749.png" alt="" />

4. In order to add a dimension/ attribute to the chart card, click **+/- attribute**. Select the desired attribute and click **Select**. You can select up to 50 attributes.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/4d0f9d5b-474b-46ee-b596-01ae8d60711c.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/96161cb8-1e51-4ea4-86b7-5f5b8adbeaec.png" alt="" />

5. To view the chart based on percentage values, click the **%** icon.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/c5c441a9-0520-48c0-bbd0-85c35e1ee1b2.png" alt="" />

6. To view the Chart Explorer, click the **Explore mode** icon. This feature enables you to use filters, dimensions, KPI variables and visualizations. To know more about explore mode, click [here](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/explore-charts-in-reports-explore-mode).

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/364cfdc8-3236-478c-93cf-b5b048c5f210.png" alt="" />

7. To view the chart details, click **View chart details**.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/40b483bf-2545-48a1-ae5e-e2ff50f4ab3b.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/dd473f98-8b18-4e4d-94c3-8233a5ba66e2.png" alt="" />

8. To view the chart in **Table** or **Pie chart** format, click on the desired icon.

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/9391e806-b819-42ef-a7e1-b15a55f7cee4.png" alt="" />

<br />

<Image src="https://usercontent.clueso.io/64d06f10-2e0e-40ec-b636-a868315fe5a6/02d973ec-735d-4f5d-8c32-5bfa47115b79/7e2c885a-ac76-48c7-8efe-dfede4a01bc6/images/bbde6436-4155-4f91-b119-4bf9ed37d795.png" alt="" />

9. To save the chart as an image file, click **More options &gt; Save as Image**.

<br />

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/109c7ec-Save_as_Image.png" />

10. To save the table as an excel file, click **More options &gt; Save as Excel**.

<br />
