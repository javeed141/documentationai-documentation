---
title: Pause and End a Loyalty Promotion
---

During a [promotion's lifecycle](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-introduction#how-does-the-promotion-lifecycle-work), you may need to either temporarily suspend it or permanently deactivate it. A maker or admin can pause an upcoming or live promotion, which temporarily deactivates it and stops it from being evaluated. A paused promotion can be resumed at any time. To permanently end a promotion, an admin can stop it. This action is irreversible and moves the promotion to the stopped state, formally archiving it.

To pause or stop a loyalty promotion, follow these steps:

1. In the promotion list view, navigate to a live loyalty promotion to pause or stop. You can use a [filter](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/loyalty-promotions-viewing-a-loyalty-promotion#sorting-and-filtering-loyalty-promotions) to view all loyalty promotions that are currently live.

2. Select the live loyalty promotion to view the promotion details.

3. Select the three-dot menu on the top right and select **Pause** to pause a live loyalty promotion or **Stop** to end a live loyalty promotion.

<br />

**You have now changed the promotion's state; it is either temporarily inactive (paused) and can be resumed later, or permanently deactivated and archived (stopped).**

<Callout kind="info" title="Notes">

* You can only pause or stop an upcoming or live loyalty promotion.
* Once a loyalty promotion is stopped, it cannot be resumed.

</Callout>
