## Flash Sale Campaign

**scenario**

A retail brand in India is planning a one-day flash sale on its app and wants to send a promotional SMS to all opted-in customers. As the organization is DLT-enabled, it must use an approved promotional template with a registered sender ID and adhere to TRAI guidelines.

**Requirements**

* Use a DLT-approved promotional SMS template.

**Solution**

To create flash sale campaign follow these steps:

1. [Configure SMS Gateway](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/configure-sms-settings#/) .
2. [Create a new Campaign](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/broadcast-campaign#/) .

<Image width="70% " src="https://files.readme.io/32d893cb7bd5c2845dda1cb4782c3b4ab01de517c99da873b9935908f90ce714-image.png" />

3. Click [+Add marketing objective](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/broadcast-campaign#set-campaign-name-duration-and-strategy) and select **Boost sales** to drive sales.

<Image src="https://files.readme.io/74043bba655f76dc9c424f3d3c13e9598b93eea827bac0d67f0f122d34cddf2c-image.png" />

4. Add the audience by [creating an audience group](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/create-audience-group).
5. In the Content section, click **Add Creative** and select **SMS** as the channel.
6. Select from the existing [uploaded SMS template](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/configure-sms-template-content#uploading-sms-template) or upload a DLT-[approved template](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/configure-sms-template-content#using-approved-templates).

<Image width="70% " src="https://files.readme.io/f097df89e13c0ec314edad253734be542e84c814653806ea0b68bdc66a3e6af5-image.png" />

7. You can add label against `{#var#}` based on the campaign requirements. To know more about the labels refer to [Supported labels across engage+](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/supported-labels-for-engagement-channels#/).

<Image width="70% " src="https://files.readme.io/228cff0ec5907b8d3461e19f12eab280d09af06870213ee2b1a103ea8d7a519d-Screenshot_2025-12-08_at_11.29.53_PM.png" />

7. Select **Save**.
8. [Schedule the campaign](https://docs.capillarytech.com/docs/schedule-message#/) based on the campaign needs.

Once the campaign is approved, it is sent out to the customers.
